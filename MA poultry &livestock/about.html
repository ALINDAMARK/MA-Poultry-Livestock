Who We Are

MA POULTRY AND LIVESTOCK was founded in 2009 as a poultry breeder farm and has grown steadily over the years to establish itself as one of the best- known brands in the Ugandan poultry industry. We are now the leading supplier of day-old chicks and poultry feeds and other related Livestock in the country. We have recently started producing other livestock feeds such as dairy and pig feeds. A commercial broiler farm was set up in 2016 which is now in full production. MA POULTRY AND LIVESTOCK is a small group of farmers based in Uganda that started as a hobby has now spiraled to a thriving popular enterprise. From humble beginnings in 1990, we built our business upon the sale of few boar goats, chickens and eggs to our neighbors and family members. Today MA POULTRY AND LIVESTOCK is still proudly a family owned and led company. From a few chickens and boar goats from our backyard, to a network that extends cross country, MA POULTRY AND LIVESTOCK is a true Ugandan success story that has since expanded countrywide. But far from losing touch with our roots, we have a proud rural and livestock network which continues to grow.
